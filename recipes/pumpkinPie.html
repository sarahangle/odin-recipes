Libby's Pumpkin Pie

Description

Even on the can of pumpkin, this recipe is called "Famous Pumpkin Pie"!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp. ground ginger
  • 1/4 tsp. ground cloves
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 can (15 oz.) Libby's 100% pure pumpkin
  • 1 can (12 fl. oz.) evaporated milk
  • 1 unbaked 9-inch deep dish pie shell

Steps

  1. Mix sugar, salt, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves in small bowl. Beaat eggs in large bowl. Stir in pumpkin and sugar-spice mixture. Gradually stir in evaporated milk.
  2. Pour into pie shell.
  3. Bake in preheated 425-degree oven for 15 minutes. Reduce temperature to 350-degrees; bake for 40 - 50 minutes or until knife inserted near center comes out clean. Cool on wire rack for 2 hours. Ser immediately or refrigerate.
